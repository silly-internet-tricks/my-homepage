Tips and Tricks

Get ready to unleash your creative potential with these handy tips!

  1. Use MutationObserver to keep pages from removing your elements.
  2. Use GM.xmlhttpRequest to get around Content Security Policies.
  3. Insert custom style elements at the end of the body.
  4. Use DOMParser when fetching HTML; that way you can get the desired elements using querySelector.
  5. You can bundle dependencies into your userscripts using webpack.
  6. The extension devtools are found in Chrome by going to extensions > manage extensions then finding Tampermonkey, clicking the details button, and finding background.html under inspect views.
